This folder is intended for your high-quality mobile app mockups.

Recommended Mockup Styles:
1. `hero-mobile.png`: An angled (isometric) or straight-on mockup of the mobile app with a transparent background.
2. `feature-pos.png`: A mockup showing the POS (Point of Sale) tablet interface.
3. `feature-qr.png`: A mockup showing the QR menu scanning experience.
